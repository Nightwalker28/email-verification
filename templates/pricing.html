Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the Free Tier?

The Free Tier gives you access to 10 individual email verifications per day. Perfect for small businesses or individuals with minimal verification needs.

How do bulk CSV uploads work?

With the Professional plan, you can upload CSV files containing hundreds or thousands of email addresses. Our system will verify them all at once and provide you with a detailed report of the results.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can upgrade to a higher plan at any time and the changes take effect immediately. You can also downgrade to a lower plan at the end of your current billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and for Professional customers, we also offer invoice billing.

Is there an API available?

Yes, API access is available with our Professional plan. The API allows you to integrate email verification directly into your applications and workflows.

What is advanced email validation?

Advanced email validation includes deeper checks such as domain reputation, mailbox existence verification, spam trap detection, and risk assessment to provide more accurate results.
